Code of conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for all participants, regardless of background or identity. Please treat others with courtesy and professionalism, and respect different viewpoints and experiences. Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or disruptive behavior are not tolerated.

If you experience or witness a concern, please contact the organisers (details on the Organizers page). Issues will be handled promptly and, when necessary, participants may be asked to leave the event.
